Where I'm from
Oh hi, world. I was born and raised in Los Angeles, CA (city proper), a magnificent town that I'm excited to call home. I left for a few years to go to school in the San Francisco Bay Area, came back to Los Angeles, left again for Minneapolis, then off to Chicago, and at long last have made my triumphant return to Los Angeles. What a long, strange trip it's been!

I couldn't be happier to be back in the city of angels, taco trucks, excessive traffic (okay, I hate the traffic), perpetual sunshine, fast-talkers, and Korean BBQ. But most importantly, I'm glad to be back in the only place in the world I've ever called home.

Currently residing in K-Town. What we lack in parking, we make up for in super amazing food!
